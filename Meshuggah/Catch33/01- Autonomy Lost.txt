song: Autonomy Lost


Tabbed by: Callum R. Macdonald <Mnemon@hotmail.co.uk>


Tuning       - F Bb Eb Ab Db Gb Bb Eb
Equipment    - Ibanez Custom Shop guitars,
               Line 6 amplification and effects,
               DR strings

Autonomy Lost serves as an introductory riff and verse to
the brooding passage of music that is Catch Thirty-Three.
The riff is quite simple, and there are not too many
strange syncopations to commit to instinct.

Watch out for the jump into the verse riff after it's
introduction, would be the only advice.
